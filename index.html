Who I am


My name is Samir Aoufia, I am 22 years old and I live in Gosselies at Belgium. I have a double nationality Algerian and Belge, I speak 2 languages French my native language and English. I'm currently in my last year of industrial computing at HelHa "Haute Ecole de Louvain en Hainaut" in Charleroi.


I spent my childhood in Gosselies, where I learned invaluable life lessons. Even though I didn't grow up in the best neighborhoods, it was an invaluable learning experience. I think it shows that even in less privileged contexts, I'm still building my career path.


I am passionate about the challenges offered by the digital world. My academic career has given me a solid grounding in software development, systems design and data analysis. My university projects, mixed theory and practice, have strengthened my understanding of to how develop an all project. Driven by a big curiosity, I keep myself informed of the latest technological advances.


My first goal is to get my bachelor's degree in industrial computing. It is the first step to getting a solid grounding in computer science. After that, I will do a master's degree in computer science at UMONS in Charleroi, because I love IT and want to learn even more. The master's degree will give me the opportunity to deepen my skills and immerse myself further in computer science.


Once I have completed my Master's degree, I will be looking for a stimulating job. I am looking for a professional environment that offers both stability and diversity, with challenges that will enable me to continue developing my IT skills. The idea of being constantly stimulated and challenged in my work is essential for me to maintain my passion and motivation to work.
