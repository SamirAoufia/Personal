Who I am


My name is Samir Aoufia, I am 22 years old and I live in Gosselies at Belgium. I have a double nationality Algerian and Belge, I speak 2 languages French my native language and English. I'm currently in my last year of industrial computing at HelHa "Haute Ecole de Louvain en Hainaut" in Charleroi, and I'm enrolled in a bridging program for my Master's in Computer Science at UMons, also in Charleroi. I work as a student employee at the Smart Gastronomy Lab.


I spent my childhood in Gosselies, where I learned invaluable life lessons. Even though I didn't grow up in the best neighborhoods, it was an invaluable learning experience. I think it shows that even in less privileged contexts, I'm still building my career path.


During my life I always like challenges. When I had to choose my future career, I looked for a field that would allow me to face new challenges every day. That's why I chose IT.


My bachelor's degree in Industrial IT provided me with a solid foundation in software development, system administration, automation and IoT. The projects I completed during my studies, which combined theory and practice, have strengthened my ability to solve problems in real scenarios.


My current goal is to get my bachelor's degree. This is the first step in my career as a computer scientist. I am also looking for an job in IT. After achieving this goal, my next goal is get a master's degree in computer science to deepen my knowledge and skills in IT, wich will open a lot of doors in the IT industry.


Once I have completed my Master's degree, I will be looking for a stimulating job. I am looking for a professional environment that offers both stability and diversity, with challenges that will enable me to continue developing my IT skills. The idea of being constantly stimulated and challenged in my work is essential for me to maintain my passion and motivation to work.
