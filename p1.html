For my first multidisciplinary project, we had to create
a game with 2 dice.

We have 2 part for the project, first we had the electronic part and in second we had the programming part.

In my groups we were 5 five and everyone had their part to do, my part was the programming part.

For begin, we find a game with 2 dice and the game was "Explain the game"...

Before coding, I do a C analysis of the game.


For the programming part, I use the C language.

For the electronic part, we use the Arduino.


For the game, we have 2 dice and 2 players.

Each player has a dice and the player who has the highest number wins.

But if the player has the same number, the player who has the highest number on the other dice wins.

And if the player has the same number on the other dice, the player who has the highest number on the other dice wins.
